The Aim of Life - Vilay

Understanding the ultimate spiritual goal

What is Vilay?

Vilay means blending or mixing of soul with God. Vilay eliminates birth and death cycle. The ultimate aim of every living being's life is Vilay and every one must accept it.

The Divine Message

God Shailja Narayan has enlightened for the welfare of human kind that a soul shall strive to achieve his aim at the earliest. She says that God has come down in this avatar to teach us the path of knowledge and 'Prem'. The ultimate condition of love or something greater than love which is meant only for one is 'Prem'. She says it is up to you to understand, accept and follow her preaching and for this there is no compulsion of any kind.

Path to Achieve Vilay

To achieve Vilay in your God following points are noteworthy:

  1. Accept any form and image of your God.
  2. Devote to your God.
  3. God must always be in your thoughts.
  4. God must be your only need.
  5. Be kind to all creatures and human beings.
  6. Accept God and belittle your Ego.
  7. God must be your only 'Prem'.
  8. God meets you through Prem only. You need not go anywhere outside. By living in your home and following your duty you can achieve your ultimate aim and God will accept you and by your Prem for God you can find HIM.
  9. God does not want your wealth in any form because he himself has given that to you.

Key Teachings

Prem (Ultimate Love)

The ultimate condition of love or something greater than love, meant only for the divine.

Spiritual Knowledge

Understanding the true nature of existence and our connection with the divine.

Devotion

Complete dedication and surrender to the divine will of God.

Kindness

Being compassionate to all creatures and human beings without discrimination.
